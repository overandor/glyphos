# Why Incall vs Outcall Massage in Manhattan: How to Choose

Incall means you come to a private, prepared space. Outcall means the session comes to you. Incall is usually better for controlled environment, equipment, and focus. Outcall works for clients who prefer their own space. In Manhattan, both have a place — the right choice depends on your schedule, privacy preference, and how much you want to travel after the session.
